## 📋 Quick Reference

Habiba Minhas content strategy targets **Pakistani women (18-45), mothers, and gift buyers** through a **100-post topical map** across **6 content pillars**, driving organic traffic to product collections.

---

## 🎯 Strategic Goals

1. **Topical Authority:** Become the trusted voice for Pakistani fashion, kids wear, and baby products
2. **Organic Traffic:** Capture long-tail search queries across all product categories
3. **Conversion Path:** Blog → Product Collection → Cart
4. **Entity Building:** Build semantic authority around Pakistani fabric, embroidery, and cultural occasions

---

## 👥 Target Audience

### **Primary Audience**
- **Pakistani women (18-45 years old)**
  - Living in Pakistan (Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad)
  - Searching in English, thinking in Urdu/Pakistani cultural context
  - Middle to upper-middle class
  - Interested in: Wedding outfits, Eid wear, formal wear

### **Secondary Audience**
- **Mothers (25-40 years old)**
  - New mothers, expecting mothers
  - Kids age 0-10
  - Searching for: Baby products, kids festive wear, nursery essentials

### **Tertiary Audience**
- **Pakistani diaspora (UK, UAE, USA)**
  - Sending gifts to Pakistan
  - Buying for occasions (weddings, Eid, baby showers)
  - Looking for authentic Pakistani fashion

## 📝 Content Format Standards

### **Structure (Every Post)**
```markdown
# [Post Title] (H1)

**Meta Description:** 120-160 chars, keyword + intent signal

## Introduction (150-200 words)
Hook + problem + preview

## [Section 1] (H2)
Detailed content with NLP-rich subheadings

## [Section 2] (H2)
...

## Conclusion
Summary + CTA

[CTA Block]
Shop [Collection Name]
```

### **Word Count**
- Target: 800-1,500 words (longer = more NLP signal density)
- Minimum: 600 words

### **Images**
- Product images from `/ladies/`, `/kids/`, `/baby/` collections
- Alt text: Entity-rich descriptions (e.g., "Embroidered silk suit for Pakistani wedding - Habiba Minhas ladies collection")

### **Schema Markup**
- Add `Article` JSON-LD schema to every journal post
- Include: headline, image, datePublished, author, publisher

## 🚀 Publishing Checklist

Before publishing any blog post:

- [ ] **H1 Tag:** Exact post title, unique on site
- [ ] **Meta Title:** Under 60 chars, includes keyword + brand
- [ ] **Meta Description:** 120-160 chars, keyword + CTA
- [ ] **Canonical URL:** Set to `https://habibaminhas.com/journal/[slug]/`
- [ ] **Word Count:** 800-1,500 words
- [ ] **Internal Links:** 1 collection page + 2-3 related posts + 1 reference
- [ ] **Images:** Optimized, descriptive alt text
- [ ] **CTA Block:** Links to relevant collection
- [ ] **Schema:** Article JSON-LD added
- [ ] **Mobile:** Preview on mobile (readable, images load)
